A landslide on Friday destroyed and rendered impassable to vehicles a portion of the national road traversing Barangay Buga in Igbaras town, Iloilo.
The road that connects Igbaras to the towns of Tubungan, Leon, and Guimbal was destroyed following heavy rains.No injuries were reported, 
but locals were waiting for remedial measures from the Department of Public Works and Highways to address transportation issues.
Igbaras Mayor Juvic Escorpion said this is the second landslide to occur in his town, following one that happened in May.
The local government is advising residents to be more cautious especially this rainy season.
